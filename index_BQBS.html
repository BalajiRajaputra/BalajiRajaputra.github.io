This bar chart is used to provide amount reimursed for each quarter by state for Medicaid program. Drop down menu has 5 options to choose. 'Q1', 'Q2', 'Q3' & 'Q4' to present reimbursed amount in each quarter and 'Year' option to show payments for entire year 2017. Select different parameters from drop down menu at the top of the scene to trigger the change to the visualization.
